Assignment 4

Contrast

SuperVillain Group
  • Homepage for a design consultancy and agency that has recently formed.
  • The first example of constrast comes from the choice of a white color for the typeface against a background
    that appears to be black at first, and when examined, is actually a black "tv static" style screen giving some
    slight action to the page and adding another contrast to the solid and static font.
  • Though not a very unique example of contrast, it's extreme "basicness" stood out to me.
  • Additionally, since this agency is trying to set themselves apart from others, there is an over all contrast of
    the simplicity and boldness of the website to the often detailed and colorful websites of other agencies.

Repetition

Manon Gruaz: Portfolio
  • The repeditive elements of this page take a moment to decifer, but when you review the entirity it seems clear.
  • The use of emoji's, especially heart emoji's, is repeated through out and gives a fun theme to the page.
  • Primary titles share a similar typeface and design, attracting the eye to each information section.

Alignment

Codecademy: Catalog

Focusing on the Catalog page for Codecademy, we see a great example of alignment to organize information and options.

To the left alignment
we have a menu of Languages and Subjects.
To the right slignment
is a box and elements for courses and subjects, all aligned on a grid.
Columns and grid
The entire page seems to be structured in four columns, and either a four or eight columned grid.

The elements are well structures and present an easy to follow hierarchy.
